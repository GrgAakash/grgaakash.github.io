SIHRS Model: System of ODEs

\[ \begin{align*} \frac{ds}{dt} &= -\beta p_{SI} s i + p_{RS} \lambda r \\ \frac{di}{dt} &= \beta p_{SI} s i - \gamma (1-p_{II}) i \\ \frac{dh}{dt} &= p_{IH} \gamma i - \alpha (1-p_{HH}) h \\ \frac{dr}{dt} &= p_{IR} \gamma i + p_{HR} \alpha h - p_{RS} \lambda r \\ \frac{dd}{dt} &= p_{ID} \gamma i + p_{HD} \alpha h \end{align*} \]

  • s: Susceptible proportion
  • i: Infected proportion
  • h: Hospitalized proportion
  • r: Recovered proportion
  • d: Death proportion
  • Œ≤, Œ≥, Œ±, Œª: rates
  • pSI: S‚ÜíI probability
  • pII: I‚ÜíI probability (remains infected)
  • pIH: I‚ÜíH probability
  • pIR: I‚ÜíR probability
  • pID: I‚ÜíD probability (death)
  • pHR: H‚ÜíR probability
  • pHH: H‚ÜíH probability (remains hospitalized)
  • pHD: H‚ÜíD probability (death)
  • pRR: R‚ÜíR probability (remains recovered)
  • pRS: R‚ÜíS probability (reinfection)
  • Constraints: pIH + pIR + pII + pID = 1, pHR + pHH + pHD = 1, pRR + pRS = 1
